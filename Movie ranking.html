Which movies to watch?

Top movies that I have watched and would recommend everyone to watch at least once in their lifetime

1. Shawshank Redemption

One of the most unique and heart touching movies I have ever watched.

2. Seven

I just love these kind of movies which are thought provoking and thrilling at the same time. I just loved Bradd Pitt and Morgan Freeman's duo.

3. The Pianist

Truly a remarkable film. Shows you how beautiful and nasty we human beings can be. How circumstances can bring the best and the worst in us.

4. 1917

An incredible film that has left an indelible mark on me.

5. Dead Poet's Society

A movie that resonates with my ideals on so many levels. To stand against the the current of orthodoxy and dogma and try to become our very own unique individual. I loved this film.
